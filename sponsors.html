Application Developers Alliance

The Application Developers Alliance is a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting software developers of every type, across all languages and platforms.

We advocate for developers on important issues relating to openness, choice, laws and legal questions, government and industry policy and building, funding, and distribution of an app. Join our partners, sponsors and individual developers contributing discounts, educational materials and event partnerships exclusive to our members.
